## Who We Are

XOGENT is an Atlanta-based Managed Security Service Provider (MSSP) and Managed Service Provider (MSP) that helps organizations reduce and mitigate risk across their IT infrastructure. We integrate security tooling, compliance management, and user education into a unified solution that makes businesses both safer and smarter.

We don't just secure your systems — we ensure your business stays compliant with evolving standards, insurable against cyber threats, and equipped with the strategic technology leadership to grow.

## What We Do

🛡️ **IT Security** — End-to-end protection including EDR, M365 hardening, penetration testing, email security, identity & access management, and 24/7 monitoring across endpoints and networks.

📋 **Compliance as a Service** — Ongoing management and maintenance of compliance with SOC 2, PCI, NIST, FINRA, HIPAA, and cyber liability insurance requirements. We keep you audit-ready year-round.

☁️ **Managed IT & Infrastructure** — Proactive monitoring, private cloud, IaaS, hosted servers, backup & disaster recovery, and full infrastructure management so you can focus on your business.

🔐 **Cyber Insurance Enablement** — We help organizations meet insurer standards from day one, reducing the risk of claim denials and higher premiums.

🧠 **Virtual CTO** — Expert IT guidance tailored to your organization, ensuring your technology strategy aligns with your business goals while maintaining security and compliance posture.

📚 **Security Training & Awareness** — Employee education programs that turn your team from a vulnerability into your first line of defense.
